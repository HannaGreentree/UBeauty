MANICURE & PEDICURE

why us

We use "e-file manicure"or "Eastern European, it also called Russian manicure. It means we use a dry cuticle technique, matte or glossy finishes, often minimalistic or with subtle accents. It brings flawless, long-lasting, clean appearance.

This style is known as the highest technical standard in the world. E-file manicure is the best for perfectionists. We remove the cuticle completely and precisely, resulting in a super clean, "pushed-back" look. Gel polish is applied extremely close to the cuticle without touching the skin, giving a “grown-out” look only after weeks.

Thanks to precision and preparation, manicures can last 3+ weeks without chipping or lifting. If you expect a "photoshopped" real-life nail look result - this is the one. Did you know that many of the world’s best nail educators are Ukrainian?! But if you prefer another manicure/pedicure style like the one you get use to just let us know, we can do it as well.
